Why Cold-Pressed Matters

Unlike refined oils that use high heat and chemicals, our cold-pressed method ensures:

  • Higher nutrient retention (vitamins E, K, and antioxidants)
  • No chemical solvents or bleaching agents
  • Authentic flavor and aroma profiles
  • Better digestion and nutrient absorption

Our Story

Raw & Real was born from a simple idea: to bring back traditional, wholesome cooking oils in their purest form.

Founded in 2018 by a team of nutritionists and food enthusiasts, we set out to challenge the mass-produced, chemically processed oils that dominate supermarket shelves.

Today, we work directly with organic farmers across India to source the finest ingredients, which we process using time-honored methods to deliver oils that are truly raw and real.
